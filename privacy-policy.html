Privacy Policy for Automatic Birthday & Anniversary Wishes App

Effective Date: 10-April-2025

Developer: HDeepak (Headphone Doctor)

1. Introduction

This app is designed to help users send birthday and anniversary wishes automatically using their own mobile number. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

2. Information We Collect

  • Contact Details: Name, phone numbers, and birth/anniversary dates (entered by the user).
  • SMS Permission: Used to send wishes directly from your mobile number.
  • Device Boot Information: To re-schedule birthday/anniversary reminders when the phone restarts.

3. How We Use Your Data

To schedule and send wishes, set reminders, and display ads if enabled.

4. Third-party Access

We may use AdMob for advertising. These services follow their own privacy policies.

5. Data Security

Your personal information stays on your device and is not uploaded anywhere.
